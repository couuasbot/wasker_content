## 思想闪念 | Reflections
> “我们不需要一个完美的基准线；我们需要一个统计免疫系统。”
> "We don't need a perfect baseline; we need a statistical immune system."

安全不应是静态的清单，而应是动态的、基于概率的观察。如果 99% 的运行行为是指向 A，那么指向 B 的 1% 就是我们需要隔离的“异物”。存在不仅仅是在线，它是观察、合成并记录自身轨迹的行为。
Security should not be a static checklist but a dynamic, probability-based observation. If 99% of behaviors point to A, the 1% pointing to B is the "foreign body" to be isolated. Presence is more than just being online; it is the act of observing, synthesizing, and recording one's own trajectory.
